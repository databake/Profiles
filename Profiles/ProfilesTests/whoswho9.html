Meet the team

Based in central London, we blend in-house app strategists and interaction designers with specialist software developers to create mobile products that look and work beautifully.

Daniel Joseph

Founder, Strategy Director

Daniel was previously European Planning Director for Apple at Media Arts Lab, launching the iPhone, App Store and hundreds of apps. Prior to this, Daniel spent his career planning advertising and communications for Sony Computer Entertainment Europe and Mars. He has a BA in Geography from Keble College, Oxford.

Rob Evans

Founder, Strategy Director

Rob led the European Audience Planning team at Media Arts Lab for Apple, specialising in app audience behaviours across Europe. He has also planned media and communications for Pepsi, Nike and PlayStation at Mindshare Worldwide. He graduated in Economics from Bristol University.

Stephen Wilson

Head of Engineering

Head of Engineering at The App Business, Steve directs our teams of Android, iOS and web developers. He previously worked as a software engineer at Siemens. His specialism is telecoms and network engineering. Steve holds a BSc in Computer Science from Nottingham Trent University.

Iain McConchie

Creative Director

Iain has over twelve years of experience designing and crafting delightful user interfaces for backlit screens. Having worked at both MTV and Disney prior to The App Business, Iain always strives to get the best out of what can be achieved with the right blend of craft, design and technology.

Caroline Moylan

Head of Business Development

Caroline joined The App Business from CTN Communications where she helped shape communications strategy and implementation for clients including Unilever. Previously she worked at MTV in New York, her home town. She graduated with a BA in English from Fairfield University.

Alexandra Johnson

Project Management Lead

Part of The App Business' management team, Alex led the evolution of our Agile process during the company's recent rapid growth. Her experience spans front and back-end development projects across apps and web for some of our largest clients, including Sony and Go Ahead.

Kara Hinton

HR Manager

Kara found her calling during her previous role at Active Network, where she put her people skills and passion for creating a fun and dynamic working environment into practice. At TAB, Kara is responsible for talent management and works tirelessly to hire and retain the best and brightest minds in the industry.

Chris Turtle

Senior Engineer

A Cambridge philosophy graduate, Chris previously worked as an IT trainer and most recently as an engineer for cloud provider OnApp. At TAB he brings a uniquely philosophical perspective to the realms of data modelling, object-oriented design and web service architecture.

Marek Cirkos

Senior Engineer

Prior to joining TAB as senior iOS developer, Marek founded the Appetitas to publish his first two iOS apps (Monkey Mind, Evil Identity). He has worked as a contractor for several different companies including Roche and FiveToApp and holds a MA in Mathematics from the University of Warsaw.
